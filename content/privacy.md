## Overview

I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how I collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit this website or engage my services.

## Information I Collect

### Information You Provide

When you contact me through this website or engage my services, I may collect:

- **Contact information**: Name, email address, organization
- **Communication content**: Messages, project requirements, and other information you share with me
- **Professional information**: Job title, areas of interest (when relevant to our engagement)

### Information Collected Automatically

This website may collect limited technical information:

- **Log data**: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages
- **Cookies**: I use minimal, essential cookies for website functionality

I do not currently use analytics services. If I add analytics in the future, I will update this policy and use privacy-respecting options (such as Plausible Analytics) that do not track individuals.

## How I Use Your Information

I use collected information to:

- Respond to your inquiries and provide requested services
- Communicate about projects and deliverables
- Improve this website and my services
- Comply with legal obligations

I do **not**:

- Sell your personal information
- Share your information with third parties for marketing purposes
- Use your information for automated decision-making or profiling

## Data Retention

I retain your information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy:

- **Contact form submissions**: Retained for the duration of our business relationship plus a reasonable period for follow-up
- **Project-related data**: Retained according to contractual agreements
- **Technical logs**: Automatically deleted after 30 days

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal information I hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your information
- Object to or restrict processing of your information
- Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact me at [nico@semanticly.ai](mailto:nico@semanticly.ai).
