How I Work

No black boxes

You'll understand what we're building and why. I document decisions, not just deliverables. When I leave, your team can maintain what we built.

Humans stay in control

I use AI to scale the boring parts—candidate generation, pattern matching, bulk validation. But domain experts make the real decisions, with evidence they can interrogate.

Iterative, not waterfall

We start with your actual questions, not a 50-page requirements doc. Deliver incrementally. Adjust based on what we learn.

Open by default

Where possible, I contribute tooling and standards back to the community. Your work benefits from shared infrastructure; the community benefits from yours.
